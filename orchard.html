Orchard

  1. California, especially Bakersfield area, produces almost all the commercially grown almonds.
  2. Orchards reduce climate change and environmental degradation over time.
  3. They help the economy by producing agricultural jobs.
  4. Once tree is established, it has continuous production.
  5. Pistachios grow well under hot, dry summers, and cool winters.
  6. Almond Orchards capture and store significant amounts of carbon both above and below orchard surface.
  7. Almond Orchards only use 8% of its agricultural water.
  8. Almond Trees contribute to water efficiency by producing two crops:
    • The kernels the part we eat
    • Hulls that are used for livestock feed
  9. Almonds are high in monounsaturated fats
  10. Almond trees help reduce greenhouse gases.
  11. Every stage of production affects California directly and positively (Farming, hulling and shelling, packing and trucking).
  12. Drastic price increase due to public taste change and favorability for health!
